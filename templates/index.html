Welcome to the Medical Diagnosis System — your intelligent health assistant powered by AI.

This platform is designed to help users identify early signs of common but serious health conditions such as stroke, diabetes, kidney disease, and pneumonia. By analyzing symptoms through a smart prediction model, the system provides instant feedback, along with diet, exercise, and wellness suggestions tailored to each condition. For critical cases, an emergency response option is available to guide users in real time. With built-in health insights and user-friendly design this system empowers users to take control of their health with confidence and convenience.
